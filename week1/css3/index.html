Community Bulletin

The city council is pleased to announce the schedule for the summer community festivals. This year we have curated various meetups including technology sessions, art exhibitions, and culinary workshops to cater to all age groups. We encourage all residents to participate actively and make these events a huge success. Free parking will be provided near the venues, and volunteers will be available to assist those with access requirements. Please register early to ensure you get your preferred seat since seating capacity is limited for certain sessions.
